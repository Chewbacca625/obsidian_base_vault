- Subscription-based:
	- licensing allows customers to buy shorter term subscriptions to lower the upfront costs of infrastructure purchases and reevaluate capacity at the point of renewal.
	- With subscription-based, customers can better align to OPEX or CAPEX budgets.
- With license portability, you can:
	- Move licenses between on-premises hardware clusters and Nutanix clusters in public clouds (AWS and Azure)
	- Refresh cluster hardware independent from software investments
	- Right-size cluster hardware to match new workload demands without impacting software subscription costs
